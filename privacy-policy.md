## 1. Introduction
Lokus is a mobile application that analyzes speech fluency and provides users with performance scores based on spoken audio.

We are committed to protecting user privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR.

## 2. Data We Collect

### a. Audio Data
When you use Lokus, we collect:
- Audio recordings of your voice submitted through the app

These recordings are strictly necessary to:
- Analyze speech fluency
- Generate scores and feedback
- Perform speech-to-text transcription

Audio files are stored securely on Cloudflare cloud infrastructure.

### b. Transcription Data
We may generate and store:
- Text transcriptions derived from your audio recordings

### c. Technical & Usage Data
We may automatically collect:
- App usage data
- Device type and operating system
- Crash reports and performance logs

## 3. Purpose of Processing
Your data is used only to:
- Deliver speech analysis features
- Improve accuracy and reliability
- Ensure security and prevent abuse

We do **not** use your data for advertising or profiling.

## 4. Data Storage & Security
- Audio and transcription data are stored using Cloudflare services (including object storage)
- Data is protected using industry-standard security measures
- Access is limited to authorized systems only

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion
Audio recordings and related data are stored **until you choose to delete them**.

You can delete your audio recordings directly from within the app.

Once deleted:
- The audio files are permanently removed from our databases and cloud storage
- Associated transcription data is also deleted

## 6. Data Sharing
We do **not sell personal data**.

Data may be processed only by:
- Cloud infrastructure providers (Cloudflare)
- Speech processing services required for core functionality

All processors act under strict data protection obligations.
